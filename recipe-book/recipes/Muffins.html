Muffins!

Description

Muffins are soft, moist baked goods similar to cupcakes but typically less sweet and more bread-like. They can be made with a variety of mix-ins like blueberries, chocolate chips, or nuts for endless flavor combinations.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup (1 stick) unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup sour cream or yogurt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen blueberries

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a muffin tin with paper liners.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the melted butter and sugar until well combined.
  3. Add the eggs, milk, sour cream (or yogurt), and vanilla extract. Mix until smooth.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, stirring until just combined.
  6. Gently fold in the blueberries.
  7. Divide the batter evenly among the muffin cups, filling each about 3/4 full.
  8. Bake for 18 to 22 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  9. Let muffins cool in the tin for a few minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
