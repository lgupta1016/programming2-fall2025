Fei-Fei Li

Dr. Fei-Fei Li is a computer scientist who is known for her work in artificial intelligence and computer vision. She co-founded the Stanford Human-Centered AI Institute and helped create ImageNet, a dataset that revolutionized machine learning. Through her research and advocacy, Dr. Li emphasizes building AI that is both powerful and ethical, ensuring technology benefits humanity.
